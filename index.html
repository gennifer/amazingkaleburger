          Step right up and try the Amazing Kale Burger! A tongue-titillating treat so tasty, you won't know it's good for you! Accept no substitutes folks, this vegan, gluten-free, soy-free burger is made from a unique top-secret recipe right here in Chicago, with Montalbano Farms kale, River Valley Ranch mushrooms, Majestic Nursery & Farm tomatoes, and other local, organic veggies (as soon as the Midwest weather allows!). Enjoy it on a homemade wheat bun, or gluten free on a crisp romaine leaf, with fresh Chicago Indoor Garden sprouts, house pickles and choice of spicy vegan chipotle sauce or Roasted Beetsup.

Available at the Logan Square Farmers Market on Sundays | May 18th - October 26 | 10am-3pm, under the Green Spirit big top. Also available frozen at your local natural food market or co-op! Don't delay, try some today!
